Nutella

  • Time: 5
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Hershey's Kiss 1-2 kisses
M&M's 1 fun size pack

Step 1: Melt ingredients in microwave for 30 seconds.
Step 2: Mix ingredients with spoon
Step 3: Enjoy with bread

Avocado Toast

  • Time: 5
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Avocado 1 small or medium size
Toast/Bread 1 slice

Step 1: Blend or mash avocado.
Step 2: Spread mashed avocado on bread.
Step 3: Add desired toppings, and enjoy!

Fruit Smoothie

  • Time: 5-10
  • Serves: 1-2
Ingredients Quantity
Strawberries 2-5
Fruit of your choice(optional) Quantity of your choice
Banana 1
Ice 5 cubes

Step 1: Peel or unseed any fruits that need it.
Step 2: Blend all of ingredients in a blender.
Step 3: Enjoy (straw reccomended!)
